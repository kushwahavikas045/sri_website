MACHINNING

Machining is done so as to make a piece of raw material cut into a desired final shape and size by a controlled material- removeal process. Much of modern-day machining is carried out by Computer Numerical Control (CNC) machine, in which computers are used to control the movement and operation of the mills, lathes and other cutting machines.

Types of Machining carried out at SRI

  • Cutting
  • Turning
  • Drilling
  • Boring
  • Reaming
  • Threading
  • Broaching
  • Marking
